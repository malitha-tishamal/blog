The Advanced Career Guideline System is a comprehensive role-based platform that bridges the gap between education and employment. Designed as an intelligent career management ecosystem, it connects all stakeholders in the career development process - from educational institutions to employers.

This system transforms traditional career guidance by implementing data-driven insights, intelligent matching algorithms, and seamless collaboration tools between students, lecturers, alumni, and companies.
